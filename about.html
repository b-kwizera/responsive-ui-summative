About The Student Finance Tracker

A lightweight, privacy-focused financial management tool designed specifically for students who want to take control of their spending.

Why SHOULD You Care At All?

Student Finance Tracker is a modern web application that helps students monitor their daily expenses, set spending limits, and gain insights into their financial habits. Unlike complex banking apps or spreadsheets, this tool focuses on simplicity and ease of use.

How It SUPPOSEDLY Works

  1. Add Your Transactions: Fill in the description, amount, category, and date for each expense or income.
  2. View Your Dashboard: Instantly see your total spending, most frequent categories, and spending trends.
  3. Set a Budget Cap: Go to Settings and define a monthly spending limit. The app will alert you when you're getting close or going over.
  4. Search & Filter: Use regex patterns to search through your records. For example, ^Food finds all entries starting with "Food".
  5. Export Your Data: Download your records as a JSON file anytime for backup or analysis in other tools.
