Application Settings

Customize your currency preferences and set spending limits to stay within budget.

Use 3-letter ISO currency codes (e.g., USD, EUR, GBP, CAD)
Example: If base is USD and you want to track in EUR, enter the USD to EUR rate
Leave at 0 for no cap. The dashboard will show remaining budget and alert you if you go over.

You MUST know THIS!!

Your settings are saved locally in your browser. They won't be lost when you close the tab, but they won't sync across different devices either. Use the export feature on the Dashboard to backup your data!
